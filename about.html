The Artist

Namaste, I'm Aabha Joshi

By day, I work as a banker — a world of numbers, precision and deadlines. But evenings and weekends belong to my first love: painting. What started as a childhood habit of sketching in the margins of school notebooks has grown into a lifelong practice across oil, acrylic and watercolour.

My work is rooted in Nepal — the golden light on lake water, the chaos and colour of old market squares, terraced hills after rain, and the quiet faces of the people I love. I paint what makes me pause.

Every painting in the gallery is an original, signed piece. When you buy one, it comes with a story — and I'm always happy to tell it. I also exhibit and auction selected works; see the Exhibitions page for what's coming up.
